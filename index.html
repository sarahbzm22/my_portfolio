KNOW ME!

Hi! I'm Sarah B., a graphic designer who loves creating designs that are not only visually stunning but also super effective. I'm currently wrapping up my second year at the University of Design, Innovation, and Technology (UDIT) in Madrid, where I’m studying graphic and multimedia design. I’ve gained experience in several areas of design and worked with tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

I'm always on the lookout for new challenges and ways to grow as a designer. My ultimate goal is to help businesses and individuals get their message across through eye-catching visuals, while continuing to improve and always putting a little piece of myself into each project.
