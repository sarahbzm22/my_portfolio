BRAND IDENTITY

REDESIGN OF EASYJET

This project involved redesigning the entire graphic and visual identity of easyJet to enhance brand coherence and communication. The goal was to ensure all design elements reflect the company’s essence as a leader in the aviation sector, while aligning with its mission, vision, and values.

The new logo, created using the "Poppins Rounded" typeface, incorporates an airplane shape on the "J" to emphasize the brand’s connection to aviation. The logo comes in several versions, including positive and negative versions in orange, white, black, and grayscale, ensuring flexibility for various applications. A protection area and minimum size guidelines were established to maintain the logo’s clarity and impact across different formats.

Primary brand colors (orange, white, and black) ensure consistency, while secondary colors (like pinks and purples) are reserved for decorative use. The brand's photography style reflects happiness, freedom, and optimism, using real, spontaneous images of people enjoying travel experiences.

The design style is minimalistic, with illustrations and typography (Poppins Rounded for titles and Futura for body text) reinforcing clarity and professionalism. Corporate stationery, digital designs, and printed materials adhere to the brand’s visual identity, promoting accessibility, simplicity, and the joy of travel.

Packaging, such as for in-flight snacks or promotional items, follows the same aesthetic, ensuring a cohesive brand experience. The redesign also extends to easyJet’s fleet and merchandising, reinforcing a modern, customer-friendly image across all touchpoints.
