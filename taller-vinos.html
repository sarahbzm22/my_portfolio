BARAJAS

I LLEGENDES

This project involved designing labels for a fictional wine brand from a Valencian vineyard. To reflect the regional identity, I based the concept on local legends and merged them with the visual language of poker cards. The brand, Barajas i Llegendes (“Decks and Legends”), replaces the traditional King, Queen, and Jack with characters from Valencian folklore, blending tradition with a refined, modern aesthetic.

The use of the Valencian language in the brand name strengthens its cultural roots, while the creative copy “In every hand, a myth; in every glass, the milestone” plays with the duality between card games and the experience of wine. Each glass becomes a symbolic moment, celebrating personal stories and legendary narratives, making the act of drinking wine more memorable and meaningful.
