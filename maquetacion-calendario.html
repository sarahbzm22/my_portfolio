CALENDAR TRIBUTE TO

TATSUKI FUJIMOTO

This was the first project assigned in our Layout Design course, which consisted of choosing a character from a given list and designing a calendar as a tribute to them. We selected Tatsuki Fujimoto, the creator of Chainsaw Man, and illustrated the main characters from the manga throughout the calendar.

Our concept was to integrate lines from songs that represented each month both thematically and visually, especially in relation to key festivities. These phrases were used as titles and were carefully chosen to match the mood of the illustrations, which combined traditional holiday elements with the visual identity of Chainsaw Man. We maintained the characters’ original style to appeal to our target audience—anime and manga fans aged 15 to 35.

To make the calendar unique, we included seasonal accessories for each character according to the month’s celebration. Each illustration was designed to be immediately recognizable without the need for titles, using visual symbolism to express the spirit of the holiday. The layout, typography choices, and overall structure were all designed to enhance both aesthetic appeal and usability.

We also drew inspiration from manga design by incorporating Japanese words, stylized titles, and familiar character positioning to guide the viewer’s attention. Through this project, we not only explored the world of manga more deeply but also gained experience using InDesign for layout, improving both our technical and conceptual design skills.
