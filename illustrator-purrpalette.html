BRAND IDENTITY DESIGN

PURRPALETTE PROJECT

This project was the final assignment for the course Digital Design I: Vector Design. The task was to create a complete brand identity for a fictional graphic design and illustration studio. I developed PurrPalette Studio, a creative company dedicated to producing distinctive illustrations and custom-made color palettes for a wide range of clients and projects.

The identity was designed to communicate a balance of creativity, warmth, and professionalism. I created a full branding system including the logo, visual elements, color palette, typography, and overall aesthetic. Every design decision aimed to reflect the essence of the studio and make it visually engaging and conceptually solid.

PurrPalette Studio is grounded in a set of core values that define its character and direction. The studio constantly strives to innovate and offer fresh, original designs that meet each project's unique needs. It maintains a strong commitment to high-quality results in every deliverable, ensuring consistency and excellence. Collaboration is at the heart of the process, with a focus on working closely with clients to bring their vision to life. And above all, passion drives every step of the creative journey, fueling both the artistic process and the outcome of each project.

This assignment brought together the technical and conceptual skills acquired throughout the course and challenged us to build a brand from scratch that felt both authentic and professional.
